Saved charts (PNG)

After you upload a CSV, the backend writes two images into the project folder static/charts/ (e.g. batch_1_category_spending.png, batch_1_monthly_trend.png). They are also served at /static/charts/... and listed in /api/summary as chart_urls.

Optional: open /charts?batch_id=2 to pin a specific upload batch (latest batch is used if you omit it).

Quick manual test

  1. Run the server: python run.py
  2. Open Swagger → try POST /api/upload with your CSV.
  3. Open this page or Dashboard — PNGs + interactive Chart.js graphs on the dashboard.
  4. Confirm files exist under static/charts/ in the project directory.
